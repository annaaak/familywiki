Steningsbo

Steningsbo is a small village i the northeast of Halmstad municipality. In Steningsbo Karl Johan Persson and Sanna Persson raised their children Peter August, Anna Charlotta, Josua Leander, Ida Linnea, Potentia Emilia, Johan Alfred and Bernhard Ludvig.

Steningsbo belongs to Torup parish.
