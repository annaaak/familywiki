Kragared

History

A small village in the north east corner of Halmstad municipality, in which Källsgård is situated in. It was here Karl Johan Larsson and Anna Charlotta Larsson moved when he came home from the United States and they married. It is then the birthplace of Astrid "Asta" Sanna Edit Lindberg, Karl Gösta Larsson and Inga Alice Hildegard Abrahamsson. When Elsie Larsson married Gösta she moved here and lived here with Gösta. Both Karl and Anna lived here until they passed, Karl in 1958 and Anna in 1992. The same holds for Elsie and Gösta, apart from their last time at nursing homes.

Kragared belongs to Enslöv parish.
