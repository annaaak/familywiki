Eskilsbo

A small village in the north east of Halmstad municipality and is noted as "Eskelsbo" mainly. This is the place where Lars Magnus Karlsson and Sanna Kristina Karlsson raised their children Svea Josefina, Karl Johan, Peter Emil, Nils Birger och Augusta Charlotta.

Eskilsbo belongs to Torup parish.
