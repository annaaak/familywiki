Stanley Nils Johnson

Born: 16 June 1916 as the second son to Nils and Svea Josefina and baby brother to Clifford Hilding.

Stanley Nils was baptised on 22 july 1917 in the Trinity Lutheran Church in Chicago. The sponsor were the brother of his mother Svea: Nils Birger. It can be noted that the family now live on Osgood street. image.

In the 1920 federal census it can be found that the family, still lives on Osgood street. Further, Stanley's maternal uncle Nils Birger is for the moment living with the family. image.

In the 1930 federal census the family has moved to Claremont street 6037, which is a house they have bought. Both Stanley and Clifford still live at home. image

During the second world war, Stanley was drafted for the army. He was a private. image,image

Stanley is mentioned in his brother's obituary in the Chicago Tribune on 30 November 1949. obituary.

Stanley's social security number was 33905130. image

Stanley passed away in Covered Bridge, Lake Worth, Florida, at March 29 1989. In the obituary his companion Jane Hudson, niece Janis, and nephews, Tom and Cliff, are mentioned. obituary
