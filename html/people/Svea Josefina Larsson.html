Svea Josefina Larsson

Life

Born: January 3 1884.

Svea Josefina moves to America in September 1902 (mistaken date?). URL image

Svea Josefina travelled on the S.S. Saxonia from Liverpool on 16 September 1902 and arrived at the port of Boston on 25 September 1902. She has named a friend Fred Johnson in Des Moines as a destination. Further, she has 10 dollars and it was her father (Lars Magnus) that paid for the ticket. image.

On the 6 June 1908 Svea Josefina marries Nils Johnson. The wedding took place in the Trinity Lutheran Church in Chicaco, Cook, Illinois. image

In the 1910 federal census it can be found that Svea Josefina and Nils lived on Racine (unclear street name) in Chicago Ward 25, Cook county, and that they have a son, Clifford, who is 11 months old. image

Clifford Hilding was born 16 may 1909 and baptised on 16 april 1914 in the Trinity Lutheran Church. It can be noted that during this time the family is reported to live at 3349 Osgood street.The sponsors are three of Svea Josefinas siblings, Karl Johan, Nils Birger and Ida Maria. image.

In the 1920 federal census it can be found that the family, with the addition of the second son Stanley, still is living on Osgood street. Further, Svea Josefinas brother, Nils Birger is for the moment living with the family. image.

In the 1930 federal census the family has moved to Claremont street 6037, which is a house they have bought. Both Clifford, who is working as a draftsman, and Stanley still live at home. image
